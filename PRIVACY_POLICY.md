# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 30, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Mini Notes ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have control over your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Mini Notes, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email Address**: Used for account identification and recovery
- **Username**: Your chosen display name
- **Password**: Stored as a secure hash (never in plain text)

### 1.2 User-Generated Content
- **Notes**: Titles, content, categories, colors, and metadata
- **Timestamps**: When you create, update, or delete content

### 1.3 Technical Data
- **Authentication Tokens**: JWT tokens for session management
- **Browser Data**: Stored locally in your browser (localStorage)

### 1.4 Data We DO NOT Collect
- Real names, phone numbers, or physical addresses
- Payment information
- Tracking cookies or analytics
- IP addresses (not logged or stored)
- Device fingerprints
- Third-party OAuth data
- Browsing history outside our application

## 2. Why We Collect Data (Legal Basis)

We collect and process your data under the following legal bases:

- **Contractual Necessity**: Email, username, and password are necessary to provide our note-taking service
- **Legitimate Interest**: Session management and security features protect your account
- **Consent**: You explicitly consent to our data practices when accepting this policy

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Service Provision

### 3.2 Security
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent abuse
- Maintain data integrity

### 3.3 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations

### 3.4 What We DON'T Do
- We do NOT sell your data

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
- **Password Encryption**: Bcrypt hashing with cost factor 12
- **Transport Security**: TLS 1.3 encryption for all connections
- **Access Control**: Database credentials secured in environment variables
- **Session Security**: HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite cookies
- **Rate Limiting**: Protection against brute force attacks

### 4.2 Storage Location
- Database servers located in [Your Region]
- Data stored in PostgreSQL database
- Backups retained for 30 days maximum

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: All personal data permanently deleted within 48 hours
- Backup tapes: Overwritten within 30 days
- Logs: Maximum 30-day retention (no personal data in logs)
